About Us

At Happy Tails, our mission is simple: to connect loving families with pets who deserve a second chance at life. Founded on the principles of compassion and care, Happy Tails has been tirelessly working to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome cats and dogs of all breeds and backgrounds. Whether it’s a playful puppy, a gentle senior cat, or a spirited stray, we see the potential in every animal and work to ensure they find their perfect match.
